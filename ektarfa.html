Ektarfa

Tu firse paas aa main zidd nahi karunga Tu firse door ja main kuch nahi kahunga Jo tere jaane se mein betha hun meh khane mein Mein kha chuka hun taane jeene se nahi marunga Jo tujhse seekha hu, tujh hi pe to likhunga Jo tujhko chahunga, tujh hi pe to mitunga jo haske aunga main fir se tere saamne Hu napasand bata diyo main fir nahi dikhunga Maine dekha tujhme saadgi rahi nahi Maine dekha tune koshishe bahut kari Kehe jo tujhko apne hathon se saja dun Hai kami tu pehne bahut Fir bhi lagta kyu saji nahi Aaj bhi aise dekhe maine daayre nahi Ke tujhse baant lun mein khudko Kehdun aa rahe nahi, hai tujhse milne Bewajah hi tha jo haske keh diya Ke hum bhi taane kha rahe nahi Jo firse dekha meri ruk chali kalam thi Dil dhadakta, aankhein bheegi, baatein tang thi Jo tujhko socha mera poora ek baras thi Jab tujhko dekha to aur bhi dikhas thi Dil to dukhta hai par jeena padhta hi hai Suraj se chaand bhi akele ladta hi hai Main kitna bhoolun kissa tera adta hi hai Jo kardein fasla to pyaar badhta hi hai Gaane to chal rahe par baat teri meri hai Chirag bujh gaye par raat teri meri hai Hua wo ek na jo sath janam ka vaada tha To iss sadi main jana kya aukat teri meri hai? Maana main sab hi kuch jeeta kuch bhi hara na Par jisko hara usko dekha fir dobara na Jo dhas gaya hun jaake raet mein main aankhon tak Tu hai samundar mujhpe boond ka sahara na Na mujhse puch mere haal kya sitaro ka Na dum tu kha ye aake noto ki deewaron ka Hai paisa kya tu chhoti baatein na kiya kar, Main bas pyar se gareeb, mujhko mol na hazaro ka Jo mujhse puchh le tu rasta baharon ka, To haske kehdu tu nazara meri aankhon ka Main jisko kosne chala hun uska naam yaad Fir bhi likh na pana dosh kaam hai gawaron ka Jo tujhko dekha aasma mein chand tha nahi Kahin pe chhup gaya ke kehta lagta nahi Isse haseen maine dekha kahi kuch ke Log yonn hi likhte rehte mujhpe aye khuda mein chand nahi Ye tere reshmi jo baal jaal sazi hai Mara nahi par jeete ji tu meri faansi hai Daba nahi gala kyun saansein meri ghut rahi Main kya hi dun saza ja teri har saza hi maafi hai Mujhe khabar nahi tu kis zuban mein razi hai Dikhe asar nahi tu kis dua mein baaki hai Agar kabhi main tere saamne se guzru, Mujhko mil tu ya nahi par teri ek nazar hi kaafi hai Ektarfa main naam bhi bana lunga Ektarfa mein naam bhi chhupa lunga Ek arsa jo beete teri yaadon mein, Main hoke mashhoor tujhpe zindagi luta dunga
